Crafts Circle

One of a kind Alice in wonderland theme steampunk wooden journal with vintage clock parts 21 x 15 cm steampunk journal with moving interlocking gears from an antique 1950 clock and brass plates.

The covers are made of hand picked cherry wood, fine sanded and finished with a high quality acrylic lacquer. On the front cover a spade symbol is carved and embedded inside it 2 interlocking rotatable gears, an ornament is carved also on the front an back covers and decorated with a textured brass plates.
